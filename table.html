Welcome to Altschool Africa

AltSchool Africa is a pioneering Nigerian edtech platform, founded in 2021 by Adewale Yusuf, Akintunde Sultan, and Opeyemi Awoyemi, committed to delivering practical, technology-driven learning experiences across the continent. Serving over 60,000 learners in more than 100 countries, the platform offers diverse diploma programs—from engineering, data, and business to creative economy courses like content creation and music business—with flexible pricing and income-sharing models to support accessibility and affordability. With strategic expansions including offices in Rwanda and Kenya, AltSchool is driven by a bold vision to equip 10 million Africans with profitable, future-ready skills by 2030, ultimately bridging the skills gap and empowering the next generation of innovators. AltSchool consists of the following schools:

  • School of Engineering
  • School of Data
  • School of Product
